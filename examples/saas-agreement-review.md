## Vendor-Favorable Terms to Watch

1. **Liability cap tied to recent fees** ‚Äî 3 months on annual contracts is very low
2. **No uptime SLA** ‚Äî Even 99.9% with credits is better than nothing
3. **Short renewal notice** ‚Äî 30 days is tight for large contracts
4. **Uncapped price increases** ‚Äî Always negotiate a cap
5. **Broad vendor termination** ‚Äî Customer should have this, not just vendor
6. **One-sided indemnification** ‚Äî Should be mutual
7. **No data export rights** ‚Äî Critical for avoiding vendor lock-in
